About Us

Compassionate Care for Your Mental Wellness

At Neema Consultancy Counseling, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality mental health care in a supportive, non-judgmental environment.

Founded in 2023, our practice has helped thousands of individuals and families navigate life's challenges. Our team of licensed therapists brings diverse expertise and a shared commitment to evidence-based, compassionate care.
